Texte de la conférence

This conference talks about the evolution of technologies and their place in our daily lives.
It explains how digital tools influence the way we work, communicate, and access information.
The speaker also addresses the questions this raises for the future, particularly in relation to artificial intelligence, and invites the audience to reflect on the use of these new technologies.
